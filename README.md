# Juvigo

Juvigo is a booking platform for holiday camps and language trips for kids and adolescents across Europe. We currently operate [in 9 countries](https://juvigo.org):

- [🇦🇹 Austria](https://juvigo.at)
- [🇧🇪 Belgium](https://juvigo.be)
- [🇫🇷 France](https://juvigo.fr)
- [🇩🇪 Germany](https://juvigo.de)
- [🇮🇹 Italy](https://juvigo.it)
- [🇳🇱 Netherlands](https://juvigo.nl)
- [🇵🇹 Portugal](https://juvigo.pt)
- [🇪🇸 Spain](https://juvigo.es)
- [🇨🇭 Switzerland](https://juvigo.ch)

Juvigo started in 2015 as an idea of merging technology with the classic idea of holiday camps and language trips for kids and carrying this great part of the tourism sector into the online age. Today, Juvigo is Europe's leading search and booking platform for holiday camps and language trips for kids and adolescents, bringing together large, specialized camp operators with smaller, family-owned associations and businesses. We've received critical acclaim from publications like the fvw, Süddeutsche Zeitung and WirtschaftsWoche.

It's also a project made with love by an international team in Berlin, Germany <3
